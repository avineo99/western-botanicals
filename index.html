Botanical Wellness

Our organic tisanes are abundant in antioxidants, natural Vitamin C, and flavonoid-rich botanicals that enhance immunity, promote collagen production, and contribute to glowing skin. Designed to nourish your vitality, our blends offer gentle support for feminine health during menopause and hormonal changes. Time to Sleep

Designed to cocoon the senses while buffering ambient noise, our 22 momme (pronounced "mommy") pure mulberry silk sleep masks boast an extra-wide blackout fit and a structured shape that molds perfectly to your face. This design helps alleviate the consistent pressure from side sleeping, which can contribute to fine lines and wrinkles. Both styles apply gentle pressure to encourage overnight lymphatic drainage and reduce puffiness.

Clinically Proven Skin Care

Exosomes, already esteemed in Korean clinical treatments, are now at the forefront of next-generation skincare and are incorporated into our specialized formulas. Expertly formulated and combined with bakuchiol, they enhance collagen production, enhance skin elasticity, and soothe sensitive or acne-prone skin. By providing plumping and hydrating benefits, they offer retinol-like effects, accelerating cell turnover and renewal, resulting in a more luminous complexion without causing dryness or irritation.
